Amy's Reviews on Romance Films

Welcome to my blog! This blog will showcase my favorite romance films.

Up

Summary

I did not watch too many Disney movies growing up, but one of the very few Disney movies I enjoyed is Up. It breaks my heart that the main character, Karl, doesn't have his wife around anymore to her health declining. He made many memories with her and has the picture of them hanged on the wall.

The Fault in Our Stars

Summary

I read the book version of The Fault in Our Stars first. When I was first watching the movie, I got really excited to see it in action for the relationship between Hazel and Gus. I find that out of all of the movies, they have the best chemsitry since they both have the empathy to understand the struggles of cancer and have similar interests.

Work It

Summary

Although this movie is under the Comedy and Musical genre, there were a ton of romance moments that I enjoyed. 18-year-old Quinn wants to get to her dream university by transforming herself into a dance champion. She would get her dance lessons from her private dance instructor named Jake. At the end of the movie, she meets with him after the competition is over and kindled their relationship.
